Visit East Java

Immerse Yourself in Its Fascinating History and Vibrant Cultural Heritage

Top three activities to do at East Java

Visit Mount Bromo in a Southeast of East Java

Experience the breathtaking landscapes of Mount Bromo in East Java. Witness stunning sunrises over dramatic volcanic scenery and enjoy an unforgettable adventure in nature

Try Delicious and Various Local Food

Indulge in the rich flavors of East Java with a variety of local delights, including sweet and savory pecel, mouthwatering sate madura, aromatic rawon, comforting soto lamongan, and many more.

Exploring Blue Fire at Ijen Crater

Discover Ijen Crater in East Java, one of only two places in the world with mesmerizing blue fire. Witness the enchanting blue flames and the stunning turquoise crater lake.
